The RESEARCH.md policy establishes four required sections: Tried & Worked, Tried & Backed Out, Environmental Constraints, and Candidates to Revisit. It defines a standard entry format (Date, Title, Body, Reference) and seeds the document with critical findings like the mem0 low-memory limitation and the macOS SQLite Homebrew rule. This policy ensures that experimental findings and architectural rationale remain durable without cluttering the main project README.md.
